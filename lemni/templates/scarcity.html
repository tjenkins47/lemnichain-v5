Lemnicoin (LEMNI) Tokenomics Plan

The theme of Lemnicoin is one of both security and scarcity. Lemnicoin is a "scarce" coin, with a very small number of coins in circulation compared to other cryptocoins.

2. Foundational Narrative & Value Proposition

The Asset: Lemnicoin is not just a currency; it is the digital representation of access rights and value accrual for the proprietary technology outlined in patent 63/788,247.

The Story: The founder's groundbreaking IP is the sole reason for Lemnicoin's existence. The tokenomics are designed to fairly compensate the founder for this foundational contribution while aligning their incentives with the long-term success of the project and all its holders.

4. Detailed Vesting Schedule

Founder Vesting (7,000,000 LEMNI): Cliff until Jan 1, 2027. Vesting continues linearly per block over 5 years, ending Jan 1, 2032.

Airdrop Vesting (700,000 LEMNI): Distributed among developers, early contributors, friends, and family. Unlocks 1/3 after 3 months, 1/3 after 6 months, 1/3 after 12 months.

Treasury & Liquidity (1,300,000 LEMNI): Used for liquidity pools (locked 2+ years), remainder managed by multi-sig wallet.

5. The Genesis Auction: A Strategic Value Discovery Event

Sealed-bid auction for 1,000,000 LEMNI to establish an initial market price and acquire strategic partners. Any unsold tokens are burned.

6. Justification of Founder Allocation (70%)

The project is built on patent 63/788,247. Founder receives 70% of supply, subject to 6-year vesting schedule. Founder’s success is aligned with long-term network value growth.

Assessment

The plan is realistic and effective for the Genesis Auction. Key strengths include transparency, long-term commitment, deflationary auction mechanics, and a market-friendly design.
