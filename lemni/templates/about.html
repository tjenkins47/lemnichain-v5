About LemniChain

This is the world’s most secure blockchain, and its base coin, LemniCoin, has the sole purpose of serving as the world's most secure crypto store of value.

LemniChain does not seek to "re-invent" the wheel. It isn't seeking to replace anything that already exists and works in the world of crypto, but rather, to provide the niche for the ultimate in cryptographic security: the ability to store crypto value in the most secure manner.

LemniChain, a French SASU backed by a premier Parisian law firm, operates in compliance with all EU crypto requirements. We offer the world’s most secure, quantum-resistant blockchain. With only 3 million initial coins initially, ultimately 10 million, LemniCoin provides investors with three powerful assets:

  1. A technologically superior product (arguably the most secure blockchain ever)
  2. Scarcity (only 3 million initial coins, ultimately 10 million by the end of 2033)
  3. Full regulatory compliance
