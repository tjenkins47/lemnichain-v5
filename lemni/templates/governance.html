Governance

On-chain voting and treasury proposal lifecycle.

How It Works

  • Community members can submit proposals to improve LemniChain.
  • Token holders vote directly on proposals using their wallet stake.
  • Approved proposals are funded by the on-chain treasury.

Proposal Lifecycle

  1. Proposal submitted via wallet dashboard
  2. Voting period opens
  3. Votes counted, quorum verified
  4. Approved proposals executed automatically
